# The Hanged Man

Suspended between worlds, The Hanged Man sees what others cannot, finding wisdom in surrender.

*"Sometimes we must let go to truly gain."*

The Hanged Man represents suspension, letting go, and sacrifice. He offers powerful resource generation at the cost of his own existence.
